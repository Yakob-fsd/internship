Membership

An online provider of entertainment (music, movies, etc.) that delivers the content via an Internet connection to the subscriber's computer, TV or mobile device. Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, Hulu, Spotify and Apple Music are high-profile examples.

Streaming Devices

Streaming media devices connect your TV or home theater to the internet, allowing you to stream content (video, music, movies and sports) through a streaming TV service provider or app. Smart TVs are essentially a television with streaming apps built in.

MEAN Stack

The MEAN stack is JavaScript-based framework for developing web applications. MEAN is named after MongoDB, Express, Angular, and Node, the four key technologies that make up the layers of the stack.
